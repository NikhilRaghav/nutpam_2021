FAQs Frequently Asked Questions

What is NUTPAM 2021?

It is a one day symposium conducted by the students of Department Computer Science and Engineering of Sri Sairam Institute of Technology.

Who can come?

Nutpam 2021 is open to any enrolled undergraduate or graduate student

When and where?

For the health and safety of our participants and , Nutpam 2021 will be held online on November 17, 2021!!!!

How much does it cost?

It’s FREE! All you need is a laptop and Wi-Fi!

How does registration work?

Registration will happen through Google Forms.

Have additional questions?
